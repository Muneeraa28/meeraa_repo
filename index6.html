"Anxiety Disorder"

An anxiety disorder is a type of mental health condition.
If you have an anxiety disorder, you may respond to certain things and situations with fear and dread. You
may also experience physical signs of anxiety, such as a pounding heart and sweating. Anxiety can stem from a combination of factors including genetics, brain
chemistry, environmental stress, and past experiences. While the exact causes are not fully understood, these elements can interact and contribute to the development
of anxiety disorders.
